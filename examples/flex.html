flex-direction

The flex-direction property specifies the direction of the flex items along the main axis.

flex-direction: row (default)

This is the default value. Items are arranged in a row, from left to right.

flex-wrap

The flex-wrap property controls whether the flex container's items are forced into a single line or can wrap onto multiple lines.

flex-wrap: nowrap (default)

Items are placed in a single line and will not wrap, even if they overflow the container.

flex: (grow) (shrink) (basis);

The flex property is a shorthand for flex-grow, flex-shrink, and flex-basis. It defines how a flex item will grow or shrink to fit the space available in its flex container.

flex: 1 (flex-grow: 1, flex-shrink: 1, flex-basis: 0%)

All items will grow and shrink equally to fill the container.
